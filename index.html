Trainings and Exercises

Training and exercises help improve physical fitness and strength, enhancing overall health and
endurance. Regular workouts also boost mental well-being and increase energy levels.

Lunges: Work the quadriceps,
glutes, and hamstrings.

Push-Up: Works the chest,
shoulders, and triceps.

Vertical Jump: Works the quadriceps,
glutes, calves, and core muscles.

Russian Twist: Works the obliques
and core muscles.

Split Squats: Work the quadriceps,
glutes, and hamstrings.

Sit-Up: Works the abdominals
and hip flexors.

BMI Calculator

A BMI calculator estimates if a person's weight is healthy for their height by providing a numerical value that categorizes them as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.
